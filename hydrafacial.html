What is HydraFacial?

HydraFacial is a non-surgical skin treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and hydrates your skin. This advanced treatment is performed with high-quality serums that are infused into your skin to reveal the most desired glow.

How is it different from regular facials?

HydraFacial uses an advanced multi-step machine to cleanse, extract, and hydrate the skin, while regular facials rely heavily on human touch.

HydraFacial works with a unique vortex-fusion delivery system to remove impurities, unclog pores, and infuse skin with nourishing serums in a single treatment.

What is Korean Glass Skin, and why do people love it?

Korean Glass Skin is a beauty trend in South Korea and has quickly become a global phenomenon. This look is characterized by clear, dewy, and luminous skin that appears smooth, plump, and radiant. It involves a strict skincare routine with several steps and applying specific products. Global beauty brands are using this trend as an opportunity to promote their products.

People, majorly women, love Korean Glass Skin because it offers a way to achieve blemish-free, glowing, flawless skin and a highly desirable plump complexion that appears brilliant and reflective, similar to glass. This K-beauty fad has gained overall vogue globally as a sign of youthfulness and beauty.

Since it can improve the skin’s texture and appearance, the market for Glass Skin is in the highland. Overall, Korean Glass Skin enhances natural beauty and boosts confidence, making it a beloved trend.

What is HydraFacial?

HydraFacial is a non-surgical skin treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and hydrates your skin. This advanced treatment is performed with high-quality serums that are infused into your skin to reveal the most desired glow.

How is it different from regular facials?

HydraFacial uses an advanced multi-step machine to cleanse, extract, and hydrate the skin, while regular facials rely heavily on human touch.

HydraFacial works with a unique vortex-fusion delivery system to remove impurities, unclog pores, and infuse skin with nourishing serums in a single treatment.

How HydraFacial Works

Experience transformation in just 40 minutes with our 5-step process

1

Cleansing

Gentle cleanse removes dirt, makeup, and surface impurities from the skin using specialized cleansing solutions.

2

Exfoliation

Mild exfoliating serum eliminates dead skin cells, uncovering a smoother and brighter complexion underneath.

3

Extraction

Special vortex-fusion tool gently extracts blackheads and other impurities from pores without discomfort.

4

Hydration

Antioxidant-rich serums containing peptides and powerful acids hydrate and nourish the skin deeply.

5

Protection

Moisturizer and sunscreen application protects the skin from damage and maintains healthy appearance.
